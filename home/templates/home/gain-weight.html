Muscle mass gain

To gain muscle mass one requires time, persistence, and discipline. The key factors for gaining muscle mass are the calorific surplus (you need to consume more calories than you burn) and a sufficient protein intake (2-3 g per kilogram of body weight). An appropriate nutrition plan with sufficient caloric intake and an appropriate ratio of macro nutrients (protein, carbohydrate and fat) will, in combination with the right training method, enable the gain of muscle mass.

Before Activity

We recommend a pre workout products. helps the body in providing energy for muscular work, thus increasing power, explosiveness and muscular endurance.

During Activity

We recommend that you ensure adequate hydration, especially in case of long-lasting intensive activities and hot temperatures. Bcaa is the best for that.

After Activity

After training, muscles can easily absorb nutrients, therefore it is advisable to provide protein in combination with carbohydrates as soon as possible after training.
