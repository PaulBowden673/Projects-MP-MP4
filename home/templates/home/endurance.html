Endurance

In endurance sports, the most important thing to take care of is adequate hydration of the body and sufficient energy intake. It is also important not to forget to consume enough protein, which is a building unit of the muscles and therefore indispensable for the recovery of micro-injuries that occur during physical strain.

Before Activity

The combination of beta-alanine and l-arginine will increase muscle endurance during long-lasting and high-intensity trainings.

During Activity

We recommend that you ensure adequate hydration, especially in case of long-lasting intensive activities and hot temperatures. BCAA is the best for that.

After Activity

We recommend a blend of proteins and carbohydrates in order to restore glycogen reserves.
